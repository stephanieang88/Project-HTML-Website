Getting Here

By Car

Travelling from Pasir Ris Drive 8, you will turn right into Pasir Ris Drive 1. Cruise along Pasir Ris Drive 1 and turn left to Pasir Ris Drive 6. Travel along Pasir Ris Drive 6 and you will reach Downtown East. From there, follow the signage to Aloha Loyang.

Travelling from from Loyang Ave, you will be connected to Pasir Ris Drive 3. Travel along Pasir Ris Drive 3 and you will reach Downtown East. From there, follow the signage to Aloha Loyang. Traffic along Pasir Ris Drive 3 can be heavy at times.

Travelling from Elias Road, you will be connected to Pasir Ris Drive 3. Travel along Pasir Ris Drive 3 and you will reach Downtown East. From there, follow the signage to Aloha Loyang. Traffic along Pasir Ris Drive 3 can be heavy at times.

By MRT Train

From Pasir Ris MRT station, we provide free shuttle service to and fro Aloha Loyang. Pls refer to the Shuttle Bus schedule.
