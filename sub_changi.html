Located in the east, Aloha Changi is a popular choice holiday destination for Singaporeans looking for a quick getaway and offers guests a home away from home.

With 49 bungalows and terraces ranging from 1 to 6 bedrooms, each chalet is uniquely designed to provide maximum comfort for the guests.

Taking a walk along Changi Beach and enjoy the sea breeze. For those feeling adventurous, there are a variety of activities available such as cycling or taking a ferry to Pulau Ubin. Learn more about our sunny island Singapore by registering for the Heritage Tours. There is never a dull moment at Aloha Changi.

Getting Here

By Car

From the Tampines Expressway (TPE), exit at Loyang Avenue. Travel along Loyang Avenue and turn left at juction with Changi Village Road. Travel along Changi Village Road until it joins with Netheravon Road.

By MRT Train

From Pasir Ris MRT station, we provide free shuttle ser vice to and fro Aloha Changi, which includes a trip with stopover at the Fairy Point Chalets. Pls refer to the Shuttle Bus schedule.
